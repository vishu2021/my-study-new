P3_GS1_200

Syllabus

  1. History of Indian Culture
    Salient aspects of Art Forms, Literature, and Architecture from ancient to modern times.

  2. Modern Indian History
    Covers significant events, personalities, and issues from A.D. 1757 to A.D. 1947.

  3. The Freedom Struggle
    Various stages and key contributions/contributors from different parts of the country.

  4. Post-Independence History
    Consolidation and reorganization within India till 1965 A.D.

  5. World History

    Events from the 18th century to mid-20th century, including:
    French Revolution (1789)
    Industrial Revolution
    World Wars
    Redraw of national boundaries
    Socialism, Nazism, Fascism, and their forms/effects on society

  6. Indian Society and Culture
    Salient features of Indian Society and its cultural aspects.

  7. Role of Women and Related Issues

    Role of women in society
    Women’s organizations
    Population and associated issues
    Poverty and developmental challenges
    Urbanization, its problems, and remedies

  8. Economic Reforms and Impact
    Liberalization, privatization, globalization (LPG) and their effects on economy, polity, and social structure.

  9. Social Issues
    Social empowerment, communalism, regionalism, and secularism.

  10. Natural Resource Distribution

    Water, soils, and forests in South and South-East Asia, with a focus on India
    Factors affecting the location of industries in India.

  11. Physical Geography
    Earthquakes, tsunamis, volcanic activity, cyclones, ocean currents, winds, and glaciers.

  12. Oceanic Resources of India
    The potential of India’s oceanic resources.

  13. Human Migration
    Refugee problems worldwide with a focus on India.

  14. Indian Sub-Continental Boundaries
    Frontiers and boundaries of the Indian sub-continent.

  15. Population and Settlements

    Types and patterns of population settlements.
    Urbanization, Smart Cities, and Smart Villages.
