P5_GS3_200

Syllabus

  1. Economic Planning in India
    Objectives, achievements, role of NITI Aayog, and
    pursuit of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  2. Poverty and Unemployment
    Issues of poverty, unemployment, social justice, and inclusive growth.
  3. Government Budgets and Financial System
    Components and structure.
  4. Agriculture
    Major crops, types of irrigation and systems, storage,
    transport, marketing of produce, and e-technology in the aid of farmers.
  5. Farm Subsidies and Food Security
    Direct/indirect subsidies, minimum support prices,
    Public Distribution System (PDS): objectives, functioning, limitations, revamping,
    buffer stocks, food security, and technology missions in agriculture.
  6. Food Processing Industries
    Scope, significance, location, upstream and downstream requirements,
    and supply chain management.
  7. Land Reforms
    Land reforms in India since independence.
  8. Liberalization and Globalization
    Effects on the economy, industrial policy changes, and their impact on industrial growth.
  9. Infrastructure
    Energy, ports, roads, airports, railways, and related developments.
  10. Science and Technology
    Developments and applications in daily life and national security,
    India’s policy, and achievements in science and technology.
  11. Indigenization and Technology Transfer
    Indigenization of technology, development of new technologies,
    dual/critical use technologies, and transfer of technology.
  12. Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
    Awareness of ICT, space technology, computers, energy resources,
    nano-technology, microbiology, biotechnology, IPR, and digital rights.
  13. Environmental Security and Ecosystems
    Conservation of wildlife, biodiversity, pollution and degradation,
    and environmental impact assessment.
  14. Disaster Management
    Disaster as a non-traditional security challenge, mitigation, and management.
  15. International Security Challenges
    Issues of nuclear proliferation, extremism causes/spread,
    communication networks, role of media/social networking,
    cyber security basics, money laundering, and human trafficking.
  16. India's Internal Security Challenges
    Terrorism, corruption, insurgency, and organized crimes.
  17. Security Forces and Defence Organizations
    Role, type, mandate of security forces, and higher defence organizations in India.
  18. Agriculture and Allied Fields
    Issues in agriculture, horticulture, forestry, and animal husbandry.
