2. Strong, Relevant Introduction (120‚Äì150 words)

3. Multi-Dimensional Body (650‚Äì700 words)

  • Social

  • Political

  • Economic

  • Ethical

  • Constitutional

  • Scientific/technological

  • Have one central idea

  • Flow logically from the previous one

  • Be balanced (no extreme opinions)

  • Indian context (policies, society, culture)

  • Current affairs (without naming too many reports)

  • History
