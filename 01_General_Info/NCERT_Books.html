    reduced confidence in the economy.

    electronic gadgets to officials in exchange for their services.

    For example:

    a person may offer a gift to a tax official to avoid paying taxes or to a government contractor to influence the award of a contract.

  3. Travel bribes:

    Travel bribes involve offering free travel or accommodation to officials to gain favors or influence their decisions.

    This could involve offering free air tickets, hotel stays, or

    other travel-related expenses to officials in exchange for their services.

    For example:

    a person may offer free travel and accommodation to a government official to influence the award of a contract or

    to expedite the processing of a document.

  4. Job bribes:

    Job bribes involve offering jobs or promotions to officials in exchange for their services.

    This could involve offering a job or promotion to a government official in exchange for

    their help in getting a contract or in expediting the processing of a document.

    For example:

    a person may offer a job to a government official's relative in exchange for their help in getting a contract.

  5. Service bribes:

    Service bribes involve offering services to officials in exchange for their services.

    This could involve offering to provide services such as

    legal, accounting, or consulting services to officials in exchange for

    their help in getting a contract or in expediting the processing of a document.

    For example:

    a person may offer legal services to a government official in exchange for their help in getting a contract.
